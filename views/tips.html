Eco-Friendly Tips

Helpful tips to reduce waste and live sustainably

Featured Eco Tip

Start a compost bin for your kitchen scraps! It reduces landfill waste and creates nutrient-rich soil for your garden.

Reduce Plastic Use

Small changes in your shopping habits can significantly reduce plastic waste:

  • Bring reusable bags when shopping
  • Use refillable water bottles instead of buying plastic ones
  • Choose products with minimal packaging
  • Buy in bulk to reduce packaging waste

These simple swaps can prevent hundreds of plastic items from entering landfills each year.

Repurpose Glass Jars

Glass jars from sauces, jams, and pickles can be transformed into useful household items:

  • Storage containers for pantry items
  • Vases for flowers
  • Candle holders
  • Organizers for small items like buttons or spices

Clean thoroughly and remove labels for the best results. Decorate with paint or ribbons for a personalized touch.

Proper Recycling Preparation

Maximize your recycling efforts with these preparation tips:

  • Rinse containers to remove food residue
  • Remove caps and lids (unless specified otherwise)
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space
  • Check local guidelines for accepted materials

Contaminated recyclables often end up in landfills, so proper preparation is crucial for effective recycling.

Composting Basics

Turn your organic waste into garden gold with these composting tips:

  • Balance "greens" (food scraps) with "browns" (dry leaves)
  • Chop materials into smaller pieces for faster decomposition
  • Turn the pile regularly to aerate
  • Keep the pile as moist as a wrung-out sponge

Avoid meat, dairy, and pet waste which can attract pests and create odors.

Extend Clothing Life

Reduce textile waste by caring for your clothes properly:

  • Wash in cold water to prevent shrinking and fading
  • Air dry instead of using the dryer
  • Repair small tears or missing buttons promptly
  • Rotate your wardrobe to reduce wear on individual items

Proper care can extend the life of your clothes by years, reducing the need for new purchases.

Upcycle Old Books

Transform old books into creative home decor:

  • Create a unique bookshelf by stacking and gluing books
  • Use pages for papercraft projects
  • Make gift wrap by cutting out interesting pages
  • Carve books into artistic sculptures

Upcycling gives old books new life while preventing them from ending up in landfills.
